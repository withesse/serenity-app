## Reliable free pools

| Pool | License type | Attribution | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pixabay Music | Pixabay Content License | Not required | Largest clean ambient pool |
| YouTube Audio Library | Mixed — filter to "no attribution required" | Per track | Download as wav/mp3, loss-less |
| Free Music Archive (FMA) | Varies — prefer CC0 / CC BY / CC BY-SA | Per license | Lots of ambient under Kevin MacLeod (CC BY) |
| Musopen | Public domain + CC | Usually not required | Classical only, occasionally useful |

## Subscription pools (if budget opens up)

- **Artlist Personal**: $14.99/mo student, $24.99/mo personal — covers App Store distribution
- **Epidemic Sound Personal**: $9/mo (podcast tier) / $17/mo (commercial) — commercial tier is the one that covers app distribution
- **Musicbed**: higher end, typically film not apps

Once subscribed, download confirmation emails + license PDFs go into
`_proofs/` so the paper trail survives the subscription lapsing.
