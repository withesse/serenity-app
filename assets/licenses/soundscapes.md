## Known-good Freesound filters for this category

When browsing Freesound, pin these filters to avoid attribution/commercial
traps:

- `license:"Creative Commons 0"` ← cleanest, no attribution required
- duration ≥ 30 min ← avoids jarring loop seams
- tag: rain / rain_on_roof / ocean_waves / crackling_fire / forest_night /
  crickets / wind_in_trees

Pixabay Sound Effects is another reliable CC0 equivalent; their own
license is effectively CC0 plus a no-trademark-abuse clause.
