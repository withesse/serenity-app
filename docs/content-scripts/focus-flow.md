# Clear Mind Focus
# 澄明专注

## Brief

A daytime focus warmup. It begins by naming distraction plainly, then narrows attention through breath, one physical point, and one clear intention for the next hour. The voice should stay steady and awake, with no sleepy softness and no motivational lift.

## TTS production notes

- Output length target: 12:00 ±45 seconds at 1× speed. If the read lands short, add only to the final silence block; do not stretch internal pauses into a sleepy cadence.
- Delivery: alert, level, practical. Keep ends of sentences clean and present. No hushed bedtime tone.
- Pronunciations that trip stock voices:
  - EN — "attention" stress on the second syllable; "outline" as noun, not verb-stressed
  - ZH — "澄明" pronounced chéng míng; "肋骨" pronounced lèi gǔ; "意图" second character tú
- Post-processing: neutral EQ, light de-essing only, and keep breaths natural rather than softened away.
