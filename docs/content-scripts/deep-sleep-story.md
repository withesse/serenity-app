# Midnight Harbour
# 午夜港湾

## Brief

A long sleep-story without plot. It places you inside a midnight harbour and keeps the focus on texture, temperature, distance, and the slow breath of the tide. The voice should narrow as it goes, with more space between phrases, until the scene is mostly holding itself and the narration is barely there.

## TTS production notes

- Output length target: 25:00 ±60 seconds at 1× speed. Preserve the long late pauses; trim or extend only the final silence bed if needed.
- Delivery: low, dry, and unperformed. Keep consonants soft. Let sentence endings fall away instead of landing firmly.
