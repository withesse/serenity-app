# Deep Work Warmup
# 深度工作预热

## Brief

A short pre-work ritual. It steadies the body, reduces the task to one clear unit, and cues an immediate start. The voice should be calm and clipped, with no pause longer than three seconds and no reward language.

## TTS production notes

- Output length target: 8:00 ±30 seconds at 1× speed. Keep all internal pauses at 3 seconds or less.
- Delivery: calm, direct, clipped. No bedtime softness and no motivational swell on the close.
- Pronunciations that trip stock voices:
  - EN — "cursor" stress on the first syllable; "nearest" should stay crisp, not softened into "near-est"
  - ZH — "眉心" pronounced méi xīn; "函数" second character shù; "推进" second character jìn
- Post-processing: neutral loudness, clean mids, minimal breath reduction, and no long reverb tail.
