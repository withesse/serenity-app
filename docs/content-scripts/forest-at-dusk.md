## TTS production notes

- No narration. No script body — see `_briefs.md`.
- Source and licence logging must be completed in [assets/licenses/soundscapes.md](../../assets/licenses/soundscapes.md) before bundling any final asset.
- Production target: seamless 60:00 render, stereo, -18 LUFS integrated, dusk ambience only.
- Content constraints: crickets as the main bed, one distant owl at most with gaps of at least 3 minutes, light wind in leaves, no daytime bird song, no close-mic'd insects, no abrupt branch snaps.
- Edit notes: keep the image wide and recessive; pull back any insect that reads too close to the ear; avoid obvious short loops in the cricket bed.
- Pronunciations that might trip stock voices if the metadata is ever read aloud:
  - EN — "dusk" with a clean final k sound; "crickets" stress on the first syllable
  - ZH — "暮色" pronounced mù sè; "森林" second character lín; "蟋蟀" pronounced xī shuài
