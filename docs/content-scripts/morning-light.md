# First Light
# 初光

## Brief

A short waking session. It begins close to the body and the room, adds a few slow stretches and breaths, then gathers one small intention for the coming hour before gently cueing the listener to stand. The tone should be bright in clarity, never loud in energy.

## TTS production notes

- Output length target: 7:00 ±30 seconds at 1× speed. Keep the rhythm bright and measured rather than hushed.
- Delivery: clear, awake, light-footed. Do not turn the last lines into a rally or a whisper.
- Pronunciations that trip stock voices:
  - EN — "daylight" keep both syllables clean; "focus" stress on the first syllable
  - ZH — "初光" pronounced chū guāng; "对焦" second character jiāo; "伸展" second character zhǎn
- Post-processing: a touch more upper-mid clarity than sleep sessions, no heavy lowpass, and keep breath noise natural.
